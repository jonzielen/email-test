Introducing: One World Observatory
 

One World Observatory has been a member of NYC & Company since its May 2015 opening. In its first six months, it welcomed more than 1.6 million visitors. Positioned on levels 100, 101 and 102 of One World Trade Center, One World Observatory gives guests seemingly endless panoramic views of New York City and surrounding waters from above 1,300 feet. The experience evokes feelings of the City's signature pride, hope and determination, along with the iconic building's strength, rebirth and resilience. One World Observatory incorporates interactive exhibits, a retail store and multiple dining options and also operates as a premium special-events space. For a limited time, visitors can purchase a Family Four Pack, which includes four standard admission tickets for $99.
