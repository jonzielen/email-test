Dear Member,

I am happy to share some good news: for the sixth year in a row, New York City welcomed a record number of visitors. In total, 58.3 million people visited New York City last year, 12.3 million of whom were international visitors.

Our industry faces various challenges, including the sustained strength of the dollar and more competition than ever from other destinations. However, last year’s record numbers prove that travelers are continuing to choose New York City.

Thank you for your partnership as we prepare for another year of hard work and success.

Fred Dixon
President & CEO
